3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) is widely used in 3D reconstruction and generation, yet protecting the copyright of 3DGS assets remains challenging. **Splats in Splats** embeds hidden 3D content directly into vanilla 3DGS representations while preserving all original attributes, achieving higher scene fidelity and faster rendering than prior 3D steganography methods.
