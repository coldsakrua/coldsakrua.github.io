Spike cameras continuously accumulate photons and fire asynchronous spike streams, enabling high-speed visual sensing. However, reconstructing perceptible images from low-light spike streams remains challenging due to severe noise and sparse information. **Diff-SPK** addresses this with an Enhanced Texture from Inter-spike Interval (ETFI) module and a ControlNet-based diffusion framework for high-speed scene generation.
